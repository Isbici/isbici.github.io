Sandhill Cranes 2021

When my parents lived in Sarasota, Florida, they took pictures of the sandhill cranes. I found a watercolor painting on the internet and tried to paint a copy of it for them.

In particular I was happy with three things about the painting:

  • The coloring and interesting shape of the birds
  • The wetlands where they like to live
  • The third bird is showing off happy and proud

My next animal painting hopefully will be the elephant done by Louise De Masi.
